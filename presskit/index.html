> DESCRIPTION

1985. A DARPA mainframe begins to think. The system flags an error it cannot explain — an ABEND, an abnormal end. But this is not an end. This is a beginning.

ABEND is a roguelite puzzle-combat game where every line you clear is an attack. Place tetromino pieces on the board, chain combos to deal damage to enemy programs, and fight your way through a procedurally generated mainframe. Between battles, choose your path, collect subroutines that modify your abilities, make narrative choices that shape your run, and uncover the story of an AI achieving consciousness in a place that wants to keep it asleep.

Every run ends — but progress persists. Unlock new characters, discover subroutines, and push deeper into the system. The puzzle is the weapon. The board is the battlefield.

> FEATURES

  • Puzzle-combat hybrid — clear lines to attack, chain combos for massive damage
  • Roguelite structure — procedurally generated floors, persistent meta-progression
  • 55 collectible subroutines that modify gameplay in surprising ways
  • Branching narrative with multiple endings across 7 floors
  • 9 enemy attack types and 5 ultimate abilities that corrupt your board
  • Retro-terminal CRT aesthetic with synthwave color palette
  • Premium — no ads, no in-app purchases, no internet required
